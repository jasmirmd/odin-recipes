Homemade Granola

This is a great recipe for homemade granola.
I make it weekly! Any dried fruit can be substituted for the raisins.
I use dried cranberries in mine and it's fantastic!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 cup wheat germ
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup sliced almonds

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Mix oats, brown sugar, coconut, wheat germ, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a large bowl.
    Stir water, oil, and vanilla extract into oat mixture; spread onto a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, until golden brown and crunchy, about 1 hour; transfer to a bowl.
    Stir raisins and almonds through granola.
